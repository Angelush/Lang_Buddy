## Definition
Se refiere a una prosa, un discurso o un estilo que es pomposo, aburrido o innecesariamente complejo; está "hinchado" de palabras que no aportan significado.

## Examples
- "I found the prose rather turgid, almost as if the author were being paid by the semicolon." — [[dialogues/en-c2-literature-01]]
- "I couldn't finish the CEO's memoir; it was turgid, filled with corporate jargon that meant absolutely nothing." — [[dialogues/en-c2-literature-01]]

## Notes
- Originalmente se usa para describir tejidos hinchados (médico).
- En el contexto literario, es un insulto fuerte para un escritor.
