## Definition
Alguien que es adamant mantiene una postura inamovible y se niega a ser persuadido o a cambiar de opinión sobre un tema.

## Examples
- "I'm adamant that we either disclose this strategy to the board or pull those reviews immediately." — [[dialogues/en-c1-work-ethics-01]]
- "Despite the pressure to launch early, the manager was adamant that we fix the security flaw first." — [[dialogues/en-c1-work-ethics-01]]

## Notes
- Es un adjetivo fuerte, no un verbo. No se dice "I adamant to", sino "I am adamant that..." o "He was adamant about the decision."
- Muy común en contextos de negociación formal.
