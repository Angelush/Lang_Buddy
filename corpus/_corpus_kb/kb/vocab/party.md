## Definition
Reunión social para celebrar algo o divertirse con amigos.

## Examples
- "He's going to come to the party tonight." — [[dialogues/en-a2-describing-people-01]]
- "Are you going to the party later?" — [[dialogues/en-a2-describing-people-01]]

## Notes
- *Party* implica una reunión social; no usar para grupos políticos (*political group*).
