## Definition
Doctrina o actitud que niega todo principio religioso, moral o significado intrínseco, asociada a la creencia de que la vida carece de propósito.

- "That's a slippery slope into aesthetic nihilism" — [[dialogues/en-c2-ai-creativity-01]]
- "The character's descent into nihilism mirrors his loss of faith in humanity"
