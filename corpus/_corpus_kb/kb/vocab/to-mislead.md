## Definition
Dar información falsa o presentar los hechos de manera distorsionada para inducir a error a una persona o a una audiencia.

## Examples
- "They said they couldn’t work for a company that misleads its audience." — [[dialogues/en-b2-news-01]]
- "Some shopping apps use deceptive prices to mislead customers during sales." — [[dialogues/en-b2-news-01]]

## Notes
- Verbo irregular: *mislead - misled - misled*.
- Es más formal que *to lie* (mentir).
