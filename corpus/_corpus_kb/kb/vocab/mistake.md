## Definition
Acción, decisión o juicio incorrecto, generalmente cometido de forma involuntaria.

## Examples
- "There has been a mistake in our system." — [[dialogues/en-b1-hotel-01]]
- "Sorry, I made a mistake with your order." — [[dialogues/en-b1-hotel-01]]

## Notes
Diferenciar de "fault" (responsabilidad). "It was my mistake" indica el error cometido; "It was my fault" indica que soy el responsable de la situación.
