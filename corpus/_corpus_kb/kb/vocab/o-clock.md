## Definition
Se usa solo para indicar horas exactas (ej. 10:00).

## Examples
- "My Zoom meeting is at ten o'clock." — [[dialogues/en-a1-numbers-time-01]]
- "It is nine o'clock." — [[dialogues/en-a1-numbers-time-01]]

## Notes
- No se usa con minutos (incorrecto: "ten-thirty o'clock").
