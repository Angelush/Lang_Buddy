## Dialogue

1 — Hello! Can I help you? — Yes, please. Do you have this shirt in blue?
2 — No, sorry. We have it in green and black.
3 — The black shirt is very nice. What size is it?
4 — This is a small. Is it okay?
5 — No, it's very small. Do you have a medium?
6 — Yes, here is a medium. It is twenty pounds.
7 — Great. Can I pay with my phone?
8 — Yes, of course. Use the app on the screen.
9 — Done! Thank you. — You're welcome. Goodbye!

## Pronunciation

- *Shirt* — /ʃɜːrt/ (be careful with the vowel sound; it's not "sheert").
- *Pounds* — /paʊndz/ (the **s** sounds like a /z/).
- *Of course* — /əv ˈkɔːrs/ (the **v** in *of* is very soft).
- *Medium* — /ˈmiː.di.əm/ (three clear syllables).
- *Twenty* — /ˈtwɛn.ti/ (in fast speech, the second **t** can disappear: "twen-nee").

## Translation

1 — ¡Hola! ¿Puedo ayudarle? — Sí, por favor. ¿Tiene esta camisa en azul?
2 — No, lo siento. La tenemos en verde y negro.
3 — La camisa negra es muy bonita. ¿Qué talla es?
4 — Esta es una (talla) pequeña. ¿Está bien?
5 — No, es muy pequeña. ¿Tiene una mediana?
6 — Sí, aquí tiene una mediana. Son veinte libras.
7 — Genial. ¿Puedo pagar con el móvil?
8 — Sí, por supuesto. Use la aplicación en la pantalla.
9 — ¡Hecho! Gracias. — De nada. ¡Adiós!

## Notes

- **(1)** *Size* refers to clothing dimensions (small, medium, large). In Spanish,
  this is *talla*. Use *size* for clothes and *number* for shoes.
- **(2)** *How much is it?* is the standard way to ask for a price. English uses
  the verb *to be* (is/are) while Spanish often uses *costar* (cuesta) or *ser*.
- **(3)** *Can I...?* is used for polite requests. *Can I help you?* is what a
  shop assistant says, and *Can I pay...?* is what the customer says.
- **(4)** Modern Payments: *Pay with my phone* is the common way to describe
  using NFC or an app like Apple/Google Pay.

## Agent cues

Anticipation prompts: line 1 (*Do you have this shirt in ___?*), line 3 (*What ___ is it?*), line 6 (*It is twenty ___*). Correction strategy: if the learner says "I have twenty pounds" to mean the price, remind them that the *shirt* is the subject: "The shirt is twenty pounds." Register is friendly and helpful. Model the linking in "Can I" /kə-naɪ/. Common ES-L1 error: saying "tally" or "talla" instead of "size".
