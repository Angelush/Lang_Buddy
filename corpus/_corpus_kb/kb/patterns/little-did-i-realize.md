## Form
Little + did + [sujeto] + realize + that + [cláusula]

## Function
Esta estructura permite resaltar la falta de conocimiento o perspicacia en una situación pasada. Al colocar "Little" al principio, se requiere la inversión del sujeto y el auxiliar (*did*), creando un efecto dramático y reflexivo.

## Examples
- "Little did I realize that 'I’ll bear it in mind' was basically code for 'I’m going to ignore this completely'." — [[dialogues/en-c1-cultural-difference-01]]
- "Little did I realize that the new streaming service would only have movies I'd already seen." — [[dialogues/en-c1-cultural-difference-01]]

## Contrasts
Es mucho más formal y dramático que simplemente decir "I didn't realize at all" o "I had no idea".

## Anticipation hint
Little did I realize that my new boss ________ actually my old colleague.
