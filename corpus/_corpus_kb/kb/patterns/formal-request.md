## Form
"Could you [verb phrase]?" o "Might I [verb phrase]?"

## Function
Se usa para pedir aclaraciones o acciones de forma profesional. "Might I" añade un extra de formalidad y deferencia.

## Examples
- "Could you elaborate on what 'pivoting slightly' entails in terms of specific features?" — [[dialogues/en-c1-negotiation-01]]
- "Might I suggest a middle ground?" — [[dialogues/en-c1-negotiation-01]]

## Contrasts
Más formal que "Can you tell me..." o "Can I suggest...". Es apropiado para el registro C1 empresarial.
