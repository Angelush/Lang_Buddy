## Form
- **Second Conditional**: Expresa una situación irreal en el presente (*If I were you, I would buy that phone*).
- **Third Conditional**: Expresa una situación irreal en el pasado (*If I had known, I would have warned you*).

## Function
Se emplean para imaginar resultados diferentes a los que ocurrieron o para expresar deseos hipotéticos.

## Examples
- "If I were a journalist there, I’d be worried about my job security." — [[dialogues/en-b2-news-01]]
- "If they had just been honest from the start, it wouldn't have turned into such a disaster." — [[dialogues/en-b2-news-01]]

## Contrasts
En español, a menudo usamos el subjuntivo para ambas situaciones. En inglés, la distinción entre tiempos verbales en la condición (*past simple* vs *past perfect*) es fundamental para diferenciar el presente del pasado.
