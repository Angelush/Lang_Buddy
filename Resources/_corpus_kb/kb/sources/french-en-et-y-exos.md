## Summary

This source contains a set of grammar exercises focused on the correct use of the French pronouns **y** and **en**. It consists of two main exercises with their corresponding corrections (corrigés), aimed at practicing the replacement of prepositional phrases and locatives.

## Pedagogical arc

This unit follows a direct "instruction -> practice -> verification" arc. It introduces the concept of pronominal replacement through a simple instruction ("Remplacez le groupe de mots en gras par y ou en") and an example, followed by two sets of sentences covering various syntactic contexts (locatives, partitives, prepositional objects).

## Notable peculiarities

The source is entirely in French, which is suitable for intermediate learners practicing direct immersion. It uses standard grammatical terminology.
