## Original
1 Here are some sentences with the verbs of the last few lessons:
2 Joan types letters all morning. (1)
3 David receives a call from a friend.
4 Joan cooks at home if she is not too tired. (2)
5 — Is your husband still working?
6 — Yes, he is not home yet.
7 Wait for him, he's extremely busy.
8 The phone is ringing. Answer it please. (3)
9 His wife works full-time,
10 but he only works part-time.
11 I can't afford a new car. They're too expensive.
12 — Let's go out to a restaurant tonight.
13 — No thanks, I'm not hungry,
14 but I am thirsty: let's go to the pub. (4)

## Translation
1 He aqui algunas frases con los verbos de las últimas lecciones :
2 Joan escribe cartas a máquina toda la mañana.
3 David recibe una llamada de un amigo.
4 Joan cocina en casa si no está demasiado cansada.
5 — ¿Está aún trabajando su marido ?
6 — Si, aún no ha llegado a casa.
7 Espérelo, está sumamente ocupado.
8 Suena el teléfono. Contesta, por favor.
9 Su mujer trabaja la jornada completa,
10 pero él trabaja sólo media jornada.
11 No puedo permitirme un coche nuevo. Son demasiado caros.
12 — Vayamos a un restaurante esta noche.
13 — No gracias, no tengo hambre,
14 pero tengo sed ; vamos al pub.

## Lesson notes
- **(1) To type:** escribir a máquina. Typewriter: máquina de escribir. Typist: mecanógrafa.
- **(2) To cook:** cocinar. Cook: cocinero/a. Cooker: cocina (aparato).
- **(3) Phone:** forma abreviada de telephone.
- **(4)** Acentuando **am** en 'I am thirsty' se obtiene el sentido de 'por el contrario'.

## Exercises
1 They never eat in a restaurant. — 2 His wife’s cooking is excellent. — 3 He is still waiting for a call from his boss. — 4 We had better buy a new car, the old one doesn't work. — 5 Learning English is only a part-time job.
