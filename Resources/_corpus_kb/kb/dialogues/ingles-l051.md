## Original
1 — Was he at home yesterday? — I think so, but I didn’t phone. (1)
2 — He called yesterday, but he didn't see me.
3 — Did he phone? — I don't think so.
These are the interrogative and negative forms in the past.
4 You do not change the verb, you simply put the auxiliary “do” into the past
5 I did not (didn't) like the food. He did not (didn't) phone. We did not (didn't) like him.
6 Questions are easy, too: Did he like it? Did she phone?
7 — Did you like the play last night? — I didn't see it. (2) (3)
8 — But did you go to the theatre? — Yes but I was so tired I closed my eyes.
9 — Tell me, was the play interesting? — I didn't understand very much, it was in Greek.
10 — Then why did you go? — I liked the main actor (4)
11 — Did he act well? — I didn't watch the acting. I looked at him, (5)
12 — What did you do after the play? — I dreamed about the actor! (6)
13 He did not, he didn't: He didn't like the play.
14 Did they...?: Did they phone you?

**PRONUNCIATION:**
1 ... dident foun. — 8 ... taiéd... klousd... ais. — 12 ... driimd baat...

## Translation
1 — ¿Estaba en casa ayer ? — Pienso que sí, pero no llamé por teléfono.
2 — Vino ayer, pero no me vió. — ¿ Llamó por teléfono ? — No creo.
3-6 (Explicación de las formas interrogativa y negativa).
7 — ¿Te gustó la obra anoche ? — No la vi.
8 — Pero ¿fuiste al teatro? — Sí, pero estaba tan cansado que cerré los ojos.
9 — Dime, ¿fué interesante la obra? — No entendí mucho, estaba en griego.
10 — Entonces ¿por qué fuiste ? — Me gustaba el actor principal.
11 — ¿Actuó bien? — No contemplé la actuación. Le miré a él.
12 — ¿Qué hiciste despues de la obra? — ¡Soñé con el actor!

## Lesson notes
- **(1)** Pronuncie la d de 'did' y la i atenuada.
- **(2)** Uso del pasado para acciones terminadas.
- **(3)** To play: actuar; a play: una obra de teatro.
- **(4)** Main: principal.
- **(5)** To act: actuar / representar.
- **(6)** To dream: soñar. Pasado irregular: dreamt.

## Exercises
1 I didn't finish it because I was disturbed. — 2 Who disturbed you? — 3 My sister and her friends invited me to go out with them. — 4 Where did you go? — 5 To the cinema, but I didn't like the film.

## Agent cues
Drill 'Did' + infinitive. This is a common error point for Spanish speakers who want to put the main verb in past form too.
